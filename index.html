ABOUT JAZZ ATTACK

Jazz Attack strives to welcome and include new and all people to create a community proud of diversity and inclusive of all ages, races, genders, ethnicities, sexualities, abilities, experiences, backgrounds, and lifestyles. We are happy to welcome dancers with all levels of experience!

Jazz Attack is run entirely by volunteers, who love to share their passion for swing dancing. We hold weekly dances on Thursday at the newly renovated Cambridge Arts Hall. In addition to the social dances, we teach both beginner and intermediate swing dancing lessons.

FIRST TIME DANCING?

Our social dances are first-timer friendly. Make sure to come an hour before the dance to take a drop-in lesson where you'll learn the basic steps. Check out the FAQ for what to expect on your first night


BEEN AROUND THE BLOCK?

We'd love to have you join our community! Want to brush up on your lindy hopping or learn a new move? We have for first-time Lindy Hoppers and those with years of experience.
