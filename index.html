ABOUT JAZZ ATTACK

Jazz Attack is run entirely by volunteers, who love to share their passion for swing dancing. We hold weekly dances on Thursday at the newly renovated Cambridge Arts Hall. In addition to the social dances, we teach both beginner and intermediate swing dancing lessons (at no additional cost, mind you!).
